NP Membership

Benefits for Nonprofit Organizations

  • No annual membership fee
  • Listing in the chamber business directory
  • Invitations to community events and outreach programs
  • Access to member newsletters and nonprofit training sessions

Bronze Membership

Bronze Level Benefits

  • Everything included in NP visibility benefits
  • Discounted admission to chamber networking events
  • Business promotion in the monthly member bulletin
  • Access to introductory business development workshops

Silver Membership

Silver Level Benefits

  • All Bronze membership benefits
  • Additional discounts on chamber events and seminars
  • Featured promotion on chamber social channels
  • Priority access to leadership and skills training sessions

Gold Membership

Gold Level Benefits

  • All Silver membership benefits
  • Homepage spotlight placement and premium directory visibility
  • Exclusive sponsorship opportunities at signature chamber events
  • Priority referrals and VIP invitations to leadership roundtables
